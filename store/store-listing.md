## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Tap or Trap is a collection of two classic suspense party games packed into one
app. Each tap could be safe... or spring the trap! Perfect for quick fun with
friends and family.

🐊 GAME 1 — Crocodile Bite
Press the crocodile's teeth one by one. Most are safe, but one "sore" tooth makes
the croc snap! Pick carefully, dare your friends, and avoid the bite.

🏴‍☠️ GAME 2 — Pirate Barrel
Push swords into the barrel one at a time. Most slots are empty, but one launches
the pirate sky-high! Will it be you who pops him out?

✨ FEATURES
• Two simple, addictive games in one app
• Easy one-tap controls — anyone can play
• Fun sound effects and colorful cartoon art
• Great as a party game, passing the phone around
• Plays fully offline — no internet needed
• No account required

Take turns, hold your breath, and find out: will you tap safely or trigger the
trap? Download Tap or Trap and let the suspense begin!
